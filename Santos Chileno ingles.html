Teresa de los Andes


The young woman who today is glorified in the Church with the title of Saint,
she is a prophet of God for the men and women of our time.

TERESA DE JESUS DE LOS ANDES, with the example of her life, she places before our
eyes the gospel of Christ, incarnated and put into practice to the
latest demands.

She is for humanity an indisputable proof that the call of Christ
to be saints, is actual, possible and true.

She rises before our eyes to demonstrate that the radicality of the
Following Christ is the only thing that is worthwhile and the only thing that makes you happy.
to the man.

Teresa de Los Andes, with the language of her intense life, confirms that
God exists, that God is love and joy, that he He he is our fullness.

She was born in Santiago de Chile on July 13, 1900.
At the baptismal font she was named Juana Enriqueta Josefina de los Sagrados Corazones Fernández Solar.
Juana received her school training from her at the school of the French nuns of the Sacred Heart.

At fourteen years of age she, inspired by God, decided to consecrate herself to Him as
She was religious, specifically, as a Discalced Carmelite.

Her wish came true on May 7, 1919, when she entered the small
Espíritu Santo monastery in the town of Los Andes, about 90 kms.
from Santiago.
On October 14 of that same year, she dressed in the Carmelite habit,
she thus beginning her novitiate with the name of Teresa of Jesus.
She knew long before that she would die young.

She sure that she would continue in eternity her mission to make known
and love God.

San Alberto Hurtado


Some biographical features On January 22, 1901, he was born in Viña del Mar
Alberto Hurtado Cruchaga.

The death of his father In his early childhood Alberto suffered a painful
loss: when he was four years old, his father died, so soon his
His family must move to Santiago, to live as "close friends" in houses of
relatives.

His mother, Ana Cruchaga, despite the difficulties, found ways to
serve the poorest in a patronage.

Alberto Hurtado and the Jesuits In 1909 Alberto entered the Colegio San
Ignacio directed by the Jesuit fathers.
Meanwhile, he continues to actively seek new ways to serve God.
and to the neighbor through apostolic works and through his own studies.

Providentially, the economic situation of the Hurtado Cruchaga family improves.
The long religious training will keep him away from his mother and the country for 11 years.
On August 24, 1933, when he was just over 32 years old, he was ordained
priest in Belgium.

On the 25th, Father Alberto Hurtado celebrates his first mass.
Christ is the reason for his life For Alberto Hurtado, Christ is simply everything:
the reason for his life, the strength to wait, the friend for whom and with whom
undertake the most arduous undertakings for the glory of God.

As a priest he feels that he is a personal sign of Christ, called to reproduce in
within him the feelings of the Master and to spread around him words
and gestures that encourage, heal and give life.

Hurtado wonders, "What would Christ do in my place?" He is revealing the
secret of the path of holiness, of his "being contemplative in action".
And when he exclaims "I'm glad, Lord, glad," he expresses his faith in the risen Christ.
